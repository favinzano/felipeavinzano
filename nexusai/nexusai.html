Personal Statement — Asylum Narrative

              

                
I declare the following statement to be true and correct to the best of my knowledge and belief. I am seeking asylum due to a well-founded fear of persecution in my home country.

                
My difficulties began in early 2022 when I was targeted for my community organization work. The paramilitary group "La Vanguardia" began to see us as a political threat because our meetings were not aligned with the ruling party.

                
On January 12, 2024, two men intercepted me and beat me. One shouted: "This is for continuing as an opposition leader. Next time you won't get up."

                
On January 14, 2024, I filed complaint No. VER-992. The officer said: "We don't get involved with those people."

                
On June 5, 2024, armed men arrived asking for my address. I fled. I received a text: "No matter where you hide, Veridia is small for us." I decided to flee to the United States.
